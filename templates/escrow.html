How Our Escrow System Works

1. Place Your Order

Choose your product and pay with cryptocurrency. Your funds are securely held in escrow, not sent directly to the vendor.

2. Vendor Ships

The vendor prepares and ships your order while your payment remains locked in escrow, ensuring they fulfill their part.

3. Receive & Release

Once you receive and verify your order, release the funds from escrow to the vendor. If there’s an issue, dispute resolution steps in.

Advantages of Using Escrow

Buyer Protection

Your money is safe until you’re satisfied with your purchase, reducing the risk of scams.

Vendor Assurance

Vendors are guaranteed payment once they deliver, fostering trust in every transaction.

Dispute Resolution

If something goes wrong, our team mediates fairly to resolve issues, protecting both parties.

Transparency

Track your escrow status at every stage, keeping you informed and in control.
