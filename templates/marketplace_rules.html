Marketplace Rules

Last Updated: {{ dated.strftime('%B %d, %Y') }}

Sydney is committed to providing a secure and fair platform for buyers and vendors. These Marketplace Rules ensure a safe trading environment and must be followed by all users. Violation of these rules may result in account suspension or termination.

1. General Conduct

All users are expected to:

  • Act in good faith and treat others with respect in all interactions.
  • Use accurate and truthful information when creating accounts or listings.
  • Maintain the confidentiality of your account credentials and PGP private keys.
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.

2. Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly forbidden on Sydney:

  • Fraud: Misrepresenting products, manipulating reviews, or engaging in scams (e.g., phishing, fake listings).
  • Illegal Goods: Selling items prohibited by international laws, including weapons, controlled substances (unless legally permitted), or stolen goods.
  • Hacking: Attempting to exploit, hack, or disrupt the platform, including DDoS attacks or unauthorized access.
  • Harassment: Threats, abusive language, or doxxing other users.
  • Money Laundering: Using Sydney to obscure the origin of funds.
  • Spam: Posting unsolicited messages or advertisements outside designated areas.

3. Vendor Responsibilities

Vendors must adhere to additional rules:

  • Accurate Listings: Provide clear, honest descriptions, pricing (in BTC), and stock levels for all products.
  • Timely Fulfillment: Ship orders within [specify timeframe, e.g., 48 hours] of payment confirmation and mark them as shipped promptly.
  • Quality Assurance: Ensure products match descriptions and meet reasonable quality standards.
  • Communication: Respond to buyer inquiries within [specify timeframe, e.g., 24 hours] using encrypted messaging.
  • Subscription: Maintain an active subscription package to list products.

4. Escrow and Transactions

Sydney uses a 2-of-3 multisig escrow system to secure transactions:

  • Buyers: Send the exact BTC amount to the multisig escrow address provided during checkout. Confirm payment once sent.
  • Vendors: Fulfill orders after payment is locked in escrow and mark them as shipped.
  • Release: Buyers must release funds within [specify timeframe, e.g., 7 days] of receiving the product, or funds remain locked.
  • Disputes: Raise disputes within [specify timeframe, e.g., 14 days] of payment if issues arise; admins will mediate using escrow keys.
  • No Direct Payments: Bypassing escrow (e.g., direct BTC transfers) is prohibited and voids platform protection.

5. Account Management

Rules governing account usage:

  • One Account: Users and vendors may maintain only one account; duplicate accounts will be suspended.
  • Security: Use strong passwords and update PGP keys if compromised.
  • Inactivity: Accounts inactive for [specify period, e.g., 6 months] may be flagged for deletion, with prior notice via messaging.

6. Enforcement and Penalties

Violations are handled as follows:

  • Warnings: Minor infractions (e.g., delayed responses) may result in a warning via encrypted message.
  • Suspension: Repeated or moderate violations (e.g., inaccurate listings) may lead to temporary account suspension.
  • Termination: Severe violations (e.g., fraud, illegal sales) will result in permanent account termination and forfeiture of escrow funds.
  • Appeals: Contact support within [specify timeframe, e.g., 7 days] of enforcement action to appeal, providing evidence.
  • Admin Discretion: Admins reserve the right to interpret and enforce these rules to protect the platform.

7. Changes to Marketplace Rules

We may update these rules as needed. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Sydney after changes indicates acceptance.

8. Contact Us

For clarification or to report violations, contact support via the messaging system or send a PGP-encrypted message to [insert admin PGP key or contact method].
