Shipping Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2023

Sydney facilitates secure transactions between buyers and vendors, with shipping handled by individual vendors. This Shipping Policy outlines the process, responsibilities, and expectations for shipping on our platform. By using Sydney, you agree to these terms.

1. Shipping Overview

Sydney does not handle shipping directly. Vendors are responsible for shipping physical goods to buyers after an order is placed and payment is secured in escrow. Shipping methods, costs, and timelines vary by vendor and are detailed in product listings or communicated via encrypted messaging.

2. How Shipping Works

The shipping process follows these steps:

  • Order Placement: Buyers place an order and send Bitcoin to the escrow address provided.
  • Payment Confirmation: Once payment is confirmed, the order status updates to "paid," and the vendor is notified.
  • Vendor Ships Item: The vendor prepares and ships the item, marking the order as "shipped" on Sydney, and may provide tracking details via encrypted messages (optional, depending on vendor policy).
  • Delivery: Buyers receive the item and confirm receipt by releasing escrow funds to the vendor.
  • Escrow Release: Funds are released to the vendor after 10 days when the buyer has confirm receive of the order.

3. Vendor Responsibilities

Vendors on Sydney are expected to:

  • Accurate Listings: Clearly state shipping costs, estimated delivery times, and methods in product descriptions.
  • Prompt Shipping: Ship items within the timeframe specified in the listing (e.g., 1-3 business days after payment confirmation).
  • Secure Packaging: Use discreet, secure packaging to protect buyer privacy and ensure safe delivery.
  • Communication: Provide updates or tracking info (if applicable) via Sydney’s encrypted messaging system.

4. Buyer Responsibilities

Buyers are responsible for:

  • Shipping Address: Providing a secure, anonymous delivery address to the vendor via encrypted messaging after payment.
  • Confirmation: Releasing escrow funds promptly upon receiving the item in satisfactory condition.
  • Disputes: Initiating a dispute within 7 days of the vendor marking the item as "shipped" if there are issues.

5. Shipping Costs and Delivery Times

Shipping costs and delivery times depend on the vendor:

  • Costs: Included in the product price or listed separately by the vendor. Review listings carefully before ordering.
  • Times: Vary by vendor location, shipping method, and destination. Typical estimates range from 3-14 business days, but check individual listings.
  • Delays: Sydney is not liable for shipping delays caused by vendors, postal services, or customs.

6. Lost or Damaged Shipments

If an item is lost or arrives damaged:

  • Contact Vendor: Buyers should first message the vendor via encrypted chat to resolve the issue.
  • Dispute Process: If unresolved, buyers can open a dispute within 7 days of the "shipped" status. Sydney admins will mediate, potentially refunding from escrow if evidence supports the claim.
  • Evidence: Buyers may need to provide proof (e.g., photos of damaged items) via encrypted messages.

7. International Shipping

Vendors may offer international shipping at their discretion. Buyers are responsible for any customs fees, duties, or legal restrictions in their country. Sydney does not intervene in customs processes.

8. Returns and Refunds

Returns are handled by vendors per their individual policies. Refunds are processed via escrow: - If an item is not delivered or arrives defective, funds may be returned to the buyer’s Bitcoin address after dispute resolution. - Once escrow is released to the vendor, refunds are between the buyer and vendor directly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Shipping Policy as needed. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Sydney implies acceptance of the revised terms.
