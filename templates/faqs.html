Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping and selling on {{ settings['site_name'] }}. We're here to help!

For Buyers

What is the escrow system and how does it protect me?

Our escrow system holds your BTC payment securely until you receive and verify your order. Once you confirm delivery, the funds are released to the vendor. If there’s an issue, our dispute resolution team steps in to ensure fairness, protecting you from scams or non-delivery.

How do I pay with Bitcoin (BTC)?

To pay with BTC, select your product and proceed to checkout. You’ll receive a unique BTC address or QR code. Send the exact amount from your wallet. Once the transaction is confirmed (usually 1 confirmation), your order is processed and held in escrow.

What happens if I don’t receive my order?

If you don’t receive your order, you can open a dispute within the delivery timeframe. Provide evidence (e.g., tracking issues), and our team will mediate. If the vendor fails to deliver, your BTC is refunded from escrow.

Are my personal details safe?

Yes! We prioritize anonymity. No personal details are required—just a username and BTC wallet. All transactions are encrypted, and we don’t store sensitive data beyond what’s needed for your account.

For Vendors

How do I advertise my products?

To advertise, deposit BTC into your vendor wallet via your unique BTC address. Then, go to the "Manage Ads" section, select a product, set a bid (BTC per click), and define a daily budget. Your product will appear as a sponsored listing in relevant categories.

How does the BTC wallet work for ads?

Your vendor wallet holds your BTC balance for ad campaigns. Send BTC to your provided address, and once confirmed (1 confirmation), it’s credited automatically. Each click deducts your bid amount from this balance until your daily budget is reached.

What happens if my ad budget runs out?

If your daily budget or wallet balance is exhausted, your ad pauses automatically. You’ll be notified, and you can deposit more BTC to resume. This ensures you never overspend beyond what you’ve funded.

How do I get paid for my sales?

Once a buyer confirms delivery, the BTC held in escrow is released to your vendor wallet. You can withdraw it to your personal BTC address at any time, minus any platform fees (if applicable). Payments are secure and instant after release.
