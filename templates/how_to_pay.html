How to Pay on Sydney

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

Paying on Sydney is secure and straightforward using Bitcoin (BTC) via our 2-of-3 multisig escrow system. Follow these steps to complete your purchase. This guide assumes basic knowledge of Bitcoin.

Step 1: Set Up a Bitcoin Wallet

Download a Bitcoin wallet (e.g., Electrum, Trust Wallet) on your device. Create a new wallet, back up your seed phrase securely, and generate a BTC address. For privacy, use a new address for each transaction.

Step 2: Acquire Bitcoin

Buy BTC from an exchange (e.g., Binance, LocalBitcoins) or a peer-to-peer service. Transfer it to your wallet. Ensure you have enough BTC to cover the product price plus a small network fee.

Step 3: Find a Product

Browse or search for products on Sydney. Click on a product to view details, then select “Buy Now” to start the checkout process. You’ll need a registered account to proceed.

Step 4: Send Payment to Escrow

After checkout, you’ll see a multisig escrow address (2-of-3: buyer, vendor, Sydney). Open your wallet, send the exact BTC amount shown (e.g., 0.001 BTC) to this address. Use the QR code for ease.

Step 5: Confirm Payment

Once sent, click “Confirm Payment” on the order page. Sydney verifies the transaction (usually within [specify timeframe, e.g., 10-30 minutes]). The order status updates to “paid” when confirmed.

Step 6: Receive Your Product

The vendor ships your order after payment is locked in escrow (status: “shipped”). Track updates via your Orders page or encrypted messages. Delivery times vary by vendor.

Step 7: Release Funds

After receiving and verifying your product, go to your Orders page and click “Release” to send BTC from escrow to the vendor. This must be done within [specify timeframe, e.g., 7 days].

Step 8: Handle Disputes (if needed)

If there’s an issue (e.g., non-delivery), click “Dispute” within [specify timeframe, e.g., 14 days]. Provide evidence via encrypted messages. Admins mediate and may refund or release funds.

Additional Tips

  • Double-Check: Verify the escrow address before sending BTC to avoid errors.
  • Fees: Include a small network fee in your wallet transaction for faster confirmation.
  • Security: Keep your wallet and PGP keys secure to protect your funds and privacy.
  • Support: Contact us via the messaging system or PGP-encrypted message to [insert admin PGP key or contact method] if you need help.
